Project Focus

This project analyzes Aadhaar enrolment trends across Indian states and age groups using publicly available UIDAI data.

Problem Statement

The goal of this project is to analyze state-wise and age-wise Aadhaar enrolment patterns to:

  • Identify enrolment trends across India
  • Detect disparities between states and age groups
  • Understand growth behavior over the years

Impact

Insights aim to help UIDAI in data-driven planning and optimization of enrolment services.

Key Insights

Demographics

Aadhaar enrolment is highest in densely populated states. Adult age groups dominate enrolment numbers.

Growth

Enrolment shows consistent growth over time with regional variations highlighting the need for targeted strategies.

Policy Implication

Focused campaigns in low-enrolment regions can increase coverage significantly.

Recommendations for UIDAI

Increase enrolment centers in high-demand states.

Strengthen awareness programs in low-enrolment regions.

Replicate successful enrolment campaigns across states.

Design initiatives for underrepresented age groups.
